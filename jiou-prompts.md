# 积偶 · 广告大片生图 Prompts

## 使用说明
1. 上传产品原图到 ChatGPT
2. 复制下方【通用前缀】
3. 选一个场景 prompt 拼在后面
4. 发送

---


## 露出层级说明（可附在任意 prompt 末尾）

**主体（Hero）** → `Product occupies center frame, sharp focus, label clearly readable, minimum 30% of frame.`

**平衡（Lifestyle）** → `Bottle clearly visible and in focus, naturally placed in scene, label partially or fully visible, no obstruction.`

**氛围（Mood）** → `Bottle present but secondary, may be partially obscured or soft focus, atmospheric presence over product clarity.`
